Prepare ingredients
  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves
Cook chicken curry
  1. saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant.
  2. Add in chicken.
  3. Continue to saute until chicken release oil.
  4. add in
    • water
    • temarind paste
    • curry leaves
    • potato
    • onion
    • suagr
    • salt
  5. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly.
  6. add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
  7. Remove and serve.
