    →How to Register?

    A. Come personally to our College

    (i). Documents / Items to bring:
    1. Original education certificates such as SPM / UEC / STPM / Diploma etc.
    2. 2 copies of passport size photographs
    3. Your IC (Identify Card)
    (ii). Please make your 1st installment or full payment at our college using...
    1. Cash OR,
    2. Cheque (payable to "Crescendo International College Sdn Bhd) OR,
    3. Credit Card OR,
    4.Touch and go

    B. For Online Registration & Online Payment

    i. Call the college to find out the fee.
    ii. Click this link to register:online registration
    IMPORTANT:
    Before registering, get ready the following:
    1. A valid email
    2. Softcopies of your photo, IC and relevant certificates (e.g. SPM, A Level, etc.) to upload
    Note: Please skip the non-compulsory questions if it is inconvenient to disclose.
    For compulsory questions (marked *) which you do not have an answer, please key-in
    the following:
    010101-00-0101 IC No. of Close Contact
    1.0 - For CGPA of Academic qualifications (e.g A Level, etc) which are not CGPA-based.
    iii. Pay fee using online payment (see below).
    iv. College will issue receipt within 3 working days (except Sat & Sun)

    Financial Aid 1. PTPTN

    Students can apply for PTPTN loan after registering in our diploma programmes or in our University Of London degree programmes. Our Diploma Coordinators or UOL Coordinators will guide you on how to apply for the loan. Please take note that the PTPTN study loan is not applicable for the A Level programme and ACCA.

    PTPTN Online Application Process

    i. Degree Programmes
    Intake: September [PTPTN application window: 1-30 August & 1-30 September]
    ii. Diploma Programmes
    Intake: January [PTPTN application window: 1-30 Dec & 1-30 Jan]
    Intake: April [PTPTN application window: 1-30 Mar & 1-30 Apr]
    Intake: September [PTPTN application window: 1-30 Aug & 1-30 Sept]

    2. EPF Account 2 Students can withdraw money from their parent's EPF Account II for their diploma programmes or for their University of London degree programmes. The EPF withdrawal, however, is not possible for the A Level programme. Please inform the Diploma Coordinators or the UOL Coordinators if you wish to apply for an EPF withdrawal.
