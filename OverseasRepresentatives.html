    About KUE

    KUE is one of East Asia's most dynamic providers of international education service. Along with CIC, KUE collaborates in designing and developing MQA approved, industry-driven training programmes such as the Diploma in Business, Graphic Design, Cabin Crew/Flight Attendant Training etc.
    KUE simultaneously facilitates student exchange between Korean Universities and CIC, in addition to organising high-value summer camps and winter camps integrated with language workshops and seminars for foreign students wishing to enjoy transnational education experience while visiting Malaysia and Singapore, or popularly known as Edutourism.
