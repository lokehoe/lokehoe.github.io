→International Student

All prospective students are advised to get in touch with our course counselors before submitting any important documents to be processed by the Admissions Department. Please check for the programme’s current availability on our website. Take into account: scheduled intakes, entry requirements, documents needed, fee structures and payment procedures. For international students, on campus hostel and transportation services are available (subject to room availability and selected routes).
NOTE: Standard procedure would be to send your certificate(s) and passport details to international@crescendo.edu.my and our International Student Unit will contact you by email. Please note that an application will only be processed upon the submission of all required documentation and payment.

For International Students

International students wishing to further their study with us should make their applications at least 3 months before the date of proposed entry. Their application can be done via electronic means, but the credentials themselves must be completed, signed and mailed prior to issuance of the necessary documents to obtain an entry visa to Malaysia.

Documentation Required

-A duly completed International Student Application Form, accompanied by a fee of RM1000 (Non-refundable).
-2 recent passport size photos (pure white background 3.5cm x 4.5cm)
-1 photocopies of passport (with all pages clear and without lines or marks)
-1 set of all certified educational certificates, transcripts and examination results (in English Translation).
-Certified copies of English Test i.e. TOEFL, IELTS, etc. (with English Translation).
Note: The College accepts the minimum score for the examination stated below:
-TOEFL (Paper Test) is 500
-TOEFL (Computer-based) is 175 -IELTS is 5.5
-Home Address, Mailing Address (if different from home address) and email address.
-Personal bond of RM 2,000 bank draft (refundable upon completion of studies), and RM2,500 bank draft (Non-refundable), made payable to CRESCENDO INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE SDN BHD as the payment for Application & Visa Processing Fees (inclusive of processing fee, medical insurance, medical screening & immigration fees). Important

-Documents must be certified by Solicitor, Commissioner of Oath, or Notary Public.
-Documents must be in English or accompanied with an English translation equivalent.
-International students aged 25 and above, the Malaysian Immigration Department required a SUPPORTING LETTER (with English Translation) from the educational institution or employer stating the actual purpose for studying in Crescendo International College. Other essential information must be declared such as: 1.International students on scholarship/study loan: To provide details.
2.International students from Sub-Sahara Africa: A No Objection Certificate (NOC) is compulsory.
3.International students from Nigeria: An Eligibility Letter (EL) is compulsory.
4.International students on Transfer/Exchange Programme: A Release Letter (RL) and Attendance Record is compulsory.
